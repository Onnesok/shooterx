ShooterX Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how ShooterX ("the Game," "we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use our mobile game. ShooterX is designed as a purely offline experience, and as such, our approach to user privacy is straightforward and transparent.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a safe and enjoyable gaming experience without concerns about data collection or sharing.

Data Collection

ShooterX is an entirely offline game. This means that we do not collect, transmit, or process any personal data or gameplay information from your device. There are no servers associated with the game for data storage or analytics.

This includes, but is not limited to, your name, email address, device identifiers, IP address, location data, gameplay statistics, or any other information that could identify you or your device.

Local Data Storage

All game progress, user preferences, high scores, and unlocked content are stored exclusively on your local device. This data is managed using standard local storage mechanisms provided by your device's operating system (e.g., SharedPreferences on Android, UserDefaults on iOS).

This local storage is not accessible by us or any third parties. Your game data remains entirely under your control on your device.

Internet & Network Usage

ShooterX is designed to be played entirely offline. It does not require an internet connection for any functionality, including gameplay, saving progress, or accessing features.

The game does not include any online features such as multiplayer modes, leaderboards, cloud saves, analytics tracking, third-party advertisements, or in-app purchases that require network access. No network permissions are requested or utilized by the game.

Sharing of Information

Since ShooterX does not collect any personal data, there is no information to share with any third parties, advertisers, or external services. Your privacy is maintained by the game's design, which eliminates any data transmission.

Children's Privacy

ShooterX is designed to be suitable for all ages and does not target children specifically for data collection. Given that no personal information is collected from any user, this policy naturally extends to children.

We adhere to the principles of the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and other relevant regulations by not collecting any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Your Control Over Data

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over your game data.

  • Deletion of Data: You can completely delete all game data, including progress, preferences, and unlocks, by uninstalling the ShooterX application from your device.
  • Resetting Progress: The game provides an option within its settings to reset your game progress and data without uninstalling the application.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes to this Privacy Policy will be reflected in future versions of the ShooterX game and will be announced through the app store listing or within the game itself (if applicable).

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Your continued use of the Game after any modifications to this Privacy Policy will constitute your acknowledgment of the modifications and your consent to abide and be bound by the modified Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please do not hesitate to contact us through the dedicated support channel provided on the ShooterX app store listing page.
